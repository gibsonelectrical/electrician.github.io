About Us

Gibson Electrical About Us

We have been in business since February of 2000 laying a foundation in residential service and contractual work, coupled with small commercial and industrial; and have grown experientially in the past six years and now contract large Commercial projects for several chain stores; such as Rite Aid, Advance Auto, Walgreen’s, Dollar General, Gahanna Event Center & Parking Garage, MECC Center (911 Center), etc. Our goal is to provide high quality customer service in a friendly, efficient, ethical, and professional manner. Our continued success depends largely on the high degree of concern we have for our customers, our professionalism and efficiency. We have a strong commitment to achieving excellence in the service and care we provide to our clients and customers. As a result of this work philosophy, we have built a strong and successful Commercial working relationship with several General Contractors; which includes: J.J.O. Construction, The Crowley Group, WXZ Construction, Hutton Construction, and Lutz Construction to name a few. These Commercial Construction Companies rely on us to represent and deliver a strong presence in the Commercial Construction field. Their continued solicitation for our business, I believe; speaks volumes about the success of our commitment to provide our clients with the best possible work ethic.

Our goal in the next five years is simple: Continue to grow and succeed using the experience, tools, and personnel to achieve the vision of our clients. By promoting with- in our Company, educating our work force on the ever changing Electrical Industry, and continuing to seek out the professional, experienced personnel needed to take our Company to the height of success in the Commercial Construction Industry. We now have individuals with over 20yrs experience in this field who have been responsible for 40 man crews, high voltage, job site safety (OSHA 30) inspections/training, Commercial/Industrial/Residential maintenance and trouble shooting. It is with the experience and professionalism of our work force that we will succeed and we fully intend on continuing to expand our knowledge and expertise by growing our company with these types of professionals. Our experience extends in to the industrial environment we can install and maintain Explosion proof requirements, 3m certified in high voltage splicing, Hipot testing available, install and maintain 4160v, motor control and design, AC/DC drives, PLC design and install, overhead crane repairs, electrical steel induction furnaces, and DC motor controls.

Specialty fields include data, voice, cctv, fire alarm, computer room environments, ups system, cell tower install and repair FFA required lighting on towers 200’and higher, and generator and auto transfer switching, whether it standby or emergency power. Again, let me thank you for the consideration for a chance to bid with your Company and the opportunity to establish ourselves as a Contractor that you rely on to provide excellence in the Commercial and Industrial Electrical fields.
